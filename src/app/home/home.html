Lets Know the Capital Cities of India

A capital city is the administrative center of a state or country where the government carries out its official functions. It often houses important government offices, legislative buildings, and administrative headquarters. India's states and union territories each have a designated capital city.
Learning about them improves geographical knowledge and provides a better understanding of India's administrative structure.

  • Kerala → Thiruvananthapuram
  • Karnataka → Bengaluru
  • Tamil Nadu → Chennai
